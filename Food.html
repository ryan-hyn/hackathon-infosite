Food Waste

Food waste is food that is discarded or not consumed, even though it is still safe to eat.

This matters because...

In the U.S., about 40% of food is wasted, costing $218 billion annually.

Wasted food releases harmful greenhouse gases when it decomposes in landfills and it contributes to climate change.

Reducing waste by just 15% could feed over 25 million people.
